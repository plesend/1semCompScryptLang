Cheng Xiaoshi

Cheng Xiaoshi (Chinese: 程小时 Chéng Xiǎoshí) is the main protagonist of the Link Click donghua series. He has the ability to dive back into photos through clapping his hands, allowing him to possess the photographer's body in order to fulfill their client's wishes. Alongside Lu Guang, he is able to share his consciousness and directly change the events of the timeline in the photo.
